Privacy Policy (Genuine Samsung Cover Customization Service)

Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. (hereinafter the “Company”) values your personal information and is committed to compliance with the “Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc.” and the “Personal Information Protection Act”.

Through this Privacy Policy, the Company informs you of what purposes and in what manner it uses the personal information received from you, and informs you of what measures are being taken to protect your privacy.

0. General Provisions

“Personal information” means information about a living individual that can be used to identify the individual concerned through data such as last name, first name, resident registration number, or social security number, etc. It can include information that can be easily combined with other information to identify a specific individual although it can’t be used to identify that individual by itself.

To ensure easy access to this Privacy Policy at all times, the Company ensures that you can read the Privacy Policy in Settings > Advanced features on your mobile phone after you put a genuine cover on it.

The Company establishes procedures to revise the Privacy Policy in order to offer continual improvement. In addition, in the event that a revision is made to the Privacy Policy, a version number is given to it so that you can easily identify the details of the revision.

This Policy shall come into force from April 1, 2018, and any revisions thereof shall be announced via a notification in Settings > Advanced features on your mobile phone.

3. Information Retention and Usage Period

In principle, your personal information shall be destroyed without delay after the purpose for which the information was collected and used is achieved.

However, if the provisions of the relevant laws and regulations make it necessary for the Company to retain your personal information, the Company may retain your personal information in accordance with the applicable provisions as described below. In such cases, the Company shall transfer the personal information to a separate database (DB) or a different storage location.

- Records related to the retraction of contracts or subscriptions : 5 years (Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc.)
- Records on payments and supply of goods, etc.: 5 years (Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc.)
- Records on the handling of consumer complaints or disputes : 3 years (Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc.)
- Records on the collection, handling, and usage of credit information : 3 years (Credit Information Use and Protection Act)
- Records on labels/advertisements : 6 months (Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc)
- Records such as the user’s Internet logs/location tracking data, etc. : 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)
- Other data used for verifying communication : 12 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

4. Procedure and Method for Destroying Personal Information

Your personal information will be destroyed using the following procedure and method.

(1) Procedure for destruction

Your personal information will be transferred to a separate DB (if in paper form, to a separate filing cabinet) after its purpose has been achieved, and will be preserved for a certain period of time in accordance with internal policies and grounds for protection of information under other relevant laws (see Retention and Use Period), and then destroyed. Personal information transferred to a separate DB shall not be used for any other purpose except for storage, unless otherwise required by the law.

(2) Method for destruction

Personal information stored in the form of electronic files shall be deleted using technological methods that disable its playback. Personal information printed on paper will be shredded or incinerated.

7. Other Policies for Processing Personal Information

(1) Technical and administrative measures to protect your privacy

The Company takes the following technical and administrative measures in processing your personal information to ensure the safety of your personal information so that it will not be lost, stolen, leaked, altered, or tampered with.

- The establishment and implementation of an internal management plan
ㆍThe Company has established and implemented an internal management plan to process your personal information safely.
ㆍThe Company checks that privacy protection measures are carried out and that the personnel in charge are working in compliance with the in-house privacy task force, etc., and ensures corrective measures are taken immediately when problems are found.

- The installation and operation of the access control system
ㆍThe Company controls unauthorized access from outside by using an Intrusion Prevention System and it is trying to equip itself with all the technical systems available to procure security system-wide.

- Measures to prevent access records from being counterfeited or tampered with
ㆍThe Company stores and manages records of access to the personal information processing system and uses security features to prevent access records from being counterfeited or tampered with.

- The encryption of personal information
ㆍYour personal information is password protected; it is stored and managed by encrypting files and transmission data or by file locking, and important data is protected by an additional security feature.

- Protection against hacking
ㆍThe Company uses antivirus programs to prevent damage by computer viruses. Antivirus programs are updated periodically, and when a new virus suddenly appears, antivirus programs are provided as soon as they become available, to prevent the breach of personal information.
ㆍThe Company has adopted an SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) that allows the secure transfer of personal information over the network by using an encryption algorithm.
ㆍEach server uses an Intrusion Prevention System, vulnerability assessment system, etc. for a full defensive preparation against external intrusion such as hacking.
ㆍPersonal information is kept separate through a separate server, not mixed with general information.

- Minimizing the number of people in charge and educating them
ㆍThe Company restricts access to the personal information of users to those who work directly with users to carry out marketing functions, those who carry out personal information management, such as personnel in charge of privacy protection, etc., and others who cannot avoid handling personal information due to the nature of their work.
ㆍThe Company provides employees who handle personal information with regular in-house training and outsourced training on new security technology, duty to protect personal information, etc.
ㆍThe Company prevents the leakage of information by people by using a security pledge given to all employees upon joining the company and has internal procedures in place to inspect the implementation of the privacy policy and employee compliance.
ㆍThe transfer of duties and responsibilities of those that handle personal information takes place while thoroughly maintaining security, and the responsibilities for privacy-related incidents that occur both after joining and leaving the company are made clear.
ㆍThe computer room, data center, and data storage room are set up as special protection areas and access to them is controlled.
