USB tethering

Use tethering to share your phone’s mobile data connection with a single computer over a USB cable.
To tether your phone connect your phone to a computer with a USB cable and on your phone tap to select “USB tethering” located under Settings >> Mobile hotspot and tethering. Tethering works with Windows®7, Windows Vista®, and Linux. For more information on tethering with operating systems, go to http://www.android.com/tether.

When you connect your phone to a computer with a USB cable, you can either share your mobile data connection by tethering OR you can share filesyou cannot do both. Do not turn on tethering if you want to use your computer to access your phone’s memory card.
